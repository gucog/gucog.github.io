Welcome to this research study on attitudes and judgments. The study takes about 10-15 minutes to complete and consists of three parts, which will all be explained as you go along.

Please note that you can cancel the study at any moment, which means that no data will be collected. Unless you want to end your participation mid-study, we ask you to make sure that you've completed the entire study before you close the window.

This study is conducted as a part of a bachelor thesis in cognitive science at the university of Gothenburg. If you have any questions about the study or are interested in the results, please send an e-mail to attitudment@gmail.com.

Thank you for your participation!
